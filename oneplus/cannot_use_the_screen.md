OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G

The left edge of the viewport had three double lines and everything becomes blurry and stuck after a second.

After that second, a line will appear on the middle of the screen and color contrast will change until a white screen of death

This is basically a cellular device WSOD.

The problem happens in the home screen (desktop screen) and in any other page I can get to.

---

Apps including the Settings app are unreachable because every time I turn off and turn on the screen and then click on the settings icon, the problem occurs.

## Notes

* Simple restart doesn't help. 
* The problem happens in power/restart/emergency mode as well.
* Holding the volume up button and the power button simultaneously, didn't cause a forced restart.
* Formatting the phone by turning off the phone and then holding the volume down button and power button simultaneously, didn't help.

## Question

How to solve that problem? Very short answer.

## Answer

This is almost certainly a hardware display or motherboard failure. 
The screen panel or motherboard likely needs replacement.

## Final conclusion

It was a screen failure, not a motherboard failure.
